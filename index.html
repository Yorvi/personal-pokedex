Yorvi Rasputin is a boy from Pallet Town, at just 10 years old he can get a starter Pokemon from Professor Oak, the towns Pokemon Expert. But, thats if Yorvi Rasputin was a regular Pokemon Trainer. Yorvi was inherited 3 Pokemon's from his late father, Legendary Gym Leader Von Rasputin. Yorvi Rasputin's dream is to be a great Pokemon Master one day and surpass his father.

This is just the beginning of the amazing adventures of Yorvi Rasputin and his Pokemon. Their journey is destined to be packed with non-stop action, millions of laughs, heart pounding perils, and endless excitement. They'll encounter fantastic friends, evil enemies, and meet creatures beyond their wildest imagination. As their story unfolds we'll unlock the magic and mystery of a most wondrous place, the Incredible World of Pokemon
